Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access a criminal record?

Authorised law enforcement personnel may access records through the secure login portal. If you identify an error in an existing record, contact our Records Department at records@rpngc.gov.pg.

When do system backups occur?

Automated backups run during off-peak hours between midnight and 2 AM each day. Each backup is typically completed within two hours and stored across multiple geographically distributed data centres.

Is the system available around the clock?

Yes. The portal maintains 99.8% availability with 24/7 access. Scheduled maintenance windows are communicated in advance and generally fall on the first Sunday of each month.

How are records protected?

Every record is encrypted using military-grade 256-bit AES encryption. Access is governed by multi-factor authentication and role-based permission tiers, with a complete audit log of every access event retained.

Can records be accessed from a mobile device?

Yes. A secure mobile application is available for both iOS and Android, allowing officers to retrieve records in the field with automatic synchronisation once connectivity is restored.

How do I report a system issue?

Contact our IT Support team at support@rpngc.gov.pg or call +675 325 0157. For security-related incidents, write to security@rpngc.gov.pg immediately.
